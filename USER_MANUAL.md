## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose of This Manual

This user manual is designed to guide you through navigating, shopping, managing your account, and using forum features on Dawn's ArtisanCraft Marketplace. Whether you're a new customer or an existing one, this manual will help you make the most of our e-commerce platform.

### 1.2 Website Overview

Dawn's ArtisanCraft Marketplace is an e-commerce platform that connects customers with talented artisans offering handcrafted products. The website offers the following core functions:

- **Product Browsing**: Browse and search through a wide selection of handcrafted products organized by categories (Jewelry, Home Decor, Textiles, Furniture, Art, Kitchenware)
- **Shopping Cart**: Add products to your cart, manage quantities, and proceed to checkout
- **Discussion Forum**: Engage with the community by creating posts and replying to discussions
- **User Account Management**: View order history, update profile information, and manage your preferences

### 1.3 Target Audience

This manual is designed for:
- New customers who are registering for the first time
- Existing customers who want to learn about additional features
- Users who need help navigating the website or troubleshooting issues

The main navigation menu is located at the top of every page. Here's what each menu item does:

- **Home**: Returns to the homepage, which displays featured products and the site banner
- **Products**: Opens the products page, where you can browse all available products organized by category
- **Forum**: Opens the discussion forum, where you can create posts and reply to community discussions
- **About**: Displays information about Dawn's ArtisanCraft Marketplace
- **Contact**: Shows contact information and a contact form

1. **Access the Products Page**
   - Click **"Products"** in the navigation menu

2. **Browse by Category**
   - Products are automatically grouped by category (Jewelry, Home Decor, Textiles, Furniture, Art, Kitchenware)

### 3.4 Discussion Forum Usage

   - Enter your response in the **"Reply"** text box at the bottom of the page

## 7. Troubleshooting

### 7.1 Common Login Issues

#### "Invalid Credentials" Error

**Problem:** You see an error message saying "Invalid email or password" when trying to log in.

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check that you are entering the correct registered email address
2. Ensure your password is correct (check for typos, extra spaces, or Caps Lock being enabled)
3. If you cannot remember your password, use the **"Forgot Password?"** feature to reset it
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies (see instructions below), then try logging in again

#### "Account Locked" Error

**Problem:** Your account has been locked after multiple failed login attempts.

**Solutions:**
1. Wait for the lockout period to expire (typically 1 hour)
2. Contact support at support@artisancraft.com to unlock your account
3. After unlocking, ensure you use the correct credentials to avoid another lockout

### 7.2 Cart/Checkout Errors

#### "Item Not Adding to Cart"

**Problem:** When you click "Add to Cart", the item doesn't appear in your cart.

**Solutions:**
1. **Clear Browser Cache and Cookies** (see instructions below)
2. Refresh the page and try adding the item again
3. Ensure you are logged into your account (some features require login)
4. Check that you have selected all required product options (if applicable)
5. Try using a different browser or device
6. If the issue persists, contact support

#### "Checkout Failed" Error

**Problem:** You see an error message when trying to place an order.

**Solutions:**
1. Verify that all mandatory shipping and billing fields are completed correctly
   - Check that your zip/postal code is valid
   - Ensure your email address is correct
2. Verify that your payment details are entered correctly (for test payments)
3. If using a mobile device, try switching to a desktop browser or vice versa
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again
5. Ensure you are logged into your account
6. Contact support if the issue persists

### 7.3 Forum Posting Problems

#### "Cannot Create Post"

**Problem:** You are unable to create a new forum post.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you are logged into your account (forum requires login)
2. Check that your post title and content are not empty
3. Verify that your post does not contain spam, offensive language, or irrelevant content
4. Try refreshing the page and submitting again
5. Contact support if the issue persists

#### "Post Not Showing Up"

**Problem:** You submitted a forum post, but it doesn't appear on the forum page.

**Solutions:**
1. Some forum posts may require moderation before being publishedâ€”wait 24 hours for approval
2. Refresh the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R) to see if your post appears
3. Check if your post was moved to a different category
4. If your post still does not appear after 24 hours, contact support to inquire
